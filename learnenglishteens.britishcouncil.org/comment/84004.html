Submitted by JoEditor on Wed, 09/25/2019 - 16:24

Hi Rohan26,
Thanks for your comment. Please be very careful if you talk to strangers online as it can be very dangerous. We have a lot of information on our website about online safety so please do read our advice. Have a look at these pages for example:
https://learnenglishteens.britishcouncil.org/skills/reading/elementary-a2-reading/online-safety-poster

https://learnenglishteens.britishcouncil.org/study-break/video-zone/i-it-safer-internet-day-song

We wouldn't call someone you've met online a 'penpal' I think it would be more common to say 'an online friend' or something similar.

Best wishes and please do take care online.

JoEditor (LearnEnglish Teens Team)
