Exams

Learn about different types of English exams and tests to do really well in your next exam.

Do you have to take English tests at school?

Are you going to do an English language exam in the future? In this section you can find tips and advice on how to get the best marks you can in all of your English tests and exams.

Choose what type of exam you want to prepare for and work through the pages. Do the exercises to check your understanding. If you're looking for general advice and tips, check out the study tips section.

Reading exams

This section gives you help and advice for all types of reading tests and exams. We explain what you can do before a reading exam and during the exam to get top marks.
