Terms and Conditions of Use

Privacy notice

The British Council will use the information that you are providing in connection with processing your registration. The legal basis for processing your information is agreement with our Terms of Use.

I understand that

  • by submitting my information through the registration form, I am consenting to the British Council collecting and using my information to inform me of its products, events and services and to carry out market research, according to the preferences I select.
  • I have the right to withdraw my consent at any time by contacting learnenglish.teens@britishcouncil.org. If I exercise this right, my personal information will be permanently deleted from the system as soon as possible.
  • If I do not provide my consent, British Council cannot collect my information.

Data protection

The British Council complies with data protection law in the UK and laws in other countries that meet internationally accepted standards.

You have the right to ask for a copy of the information we hold on you, and the right to ask us to correct any inaccuracies in that information. If you have concerns about how we have used your personal information, you also have the right to complain to a privacy regulator.

For detailed information, please refer to the privacy section of our website, www.britishcouncil.org/privacy or contact your local British Council office.

We will take steps to delete your personal information when it is no longer needed. We will remove the data of users who have not logged in for 12 months and have also not contributed comments or content to the website.

Age

You must be between 13 and 17 years old to register on this site. But please note that some countries in the EU do not allow us to collect data from children until they are 14, 15 or 16. If you are too young to register, you can still use our website to improve your English.

If you are 12 or under, you can join our website for kids. If you are 18 or over, you can join our website for adults.
