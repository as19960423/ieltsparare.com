Study break

Watch videos, read stories and articles, listen to our audio series and have fun writing about photos. Enjoy using your English!

Welcome to our Study break section. Here you can improve your everyday English and learn about the world at the same time. You will also develop your comprehension skills and build your vocabulary.

Zones: 5- to 10-minute activities

Do you have five to ten minutes to practise your English today? Try one of our video, magazine, reading or photo zone activities. These are short activities on a range of different topics to help you improve your English level. Practising every day, even for a short time, will help you to improve. Choose a topic that interests you and get started!

Series: 10- to 20-minute activities

If you have a little bit more time to work on your English, try the learning materials in our series. You will find video and audio activities which you can work through, one episode at a time, at your own speed.
