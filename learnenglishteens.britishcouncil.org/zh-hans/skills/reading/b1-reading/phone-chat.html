Submitted by JoModerator on Wed, 09/25/2019 - 09:24

Hi zfry,

Iggy says 'Yeah, my mate Barney knows it, says there's a great DJ, amazing dance music'.

The word 'mate' means the same as 'friend'. So it's Iggy's friend (Barney) who has recommended a good night club.

I hope that helps.

 

Best wishes,
