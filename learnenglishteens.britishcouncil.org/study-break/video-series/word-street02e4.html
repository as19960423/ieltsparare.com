Word on the Street

Do you want to improve your grammar and learn about the UK and its culture?

In this section, watch our Word on the Street video series for intermediate (CEFR level B1) or upper intermediate (CEFR level B2) learners. Watch clips with Ashlie and Stephen as they explore the UK, then watch teacher Rob explain the language they use.

You will improve your listening comprehension and develop your grammar and vocabulary. Each video has a transcript and interactive exercises to help you understand and use the language.
